#### **3. Compliance Checks Walkthrough (15 minutes)**

**Topics:**
- The 6 mandatory compliance checks:
  1. **3 Vendor Quotes Required**
  2. **Proper Authorization**
  3. **Budget Line Valid**
  4. **Justification Provided**
  5. **Payment Voucher Created**
  6. **Within Budget**
- How each check works
- Pass/Fail criteria
- What to look for

**Detailed Check Explanations:**

**Check 1: 3 Vendor Quotes Required**
- **Purpose**: Ensure competitive pricing
- **Pass Criteria**: ‚â•3 vendor quotes uploaded
- **Fail Criteria**: <3 quotes
- **What to verify**: Quote documents are different vendors, comparable items, recent dates

**Check 2: Proper Authorization**
- **Purpose**: Verify approval chain completed
- **Pass Criteria**: ‚â•2 approvals recorded
- **Fail Criteria**: <2 approvals or missing approver
- **What to verify**: Correct approvers for amount, all signatures present

**Check 3: Budget Line Valid**
- **Purpose**: Ensure budget allocation exists
- **Pass Criteria**: Budget line ID assigned
- **Fail Criteria**: No budget line
- **What to verify**: Correct cost centre, appropriate budget category

**Check 4: Justification Provided**
- **Purpose**: Verify business need documented
- **Pass Criteria**: >50 characters of justification
- **Fail Criteria**: ‚â§50 characters or missing
- **What to verify**: Justification makes sense, aligns with department goals

**Check 5: Payment Voucher Created**
- **Purpose**: Confirm payment processing completed
- **Pass Criteria**: ‚â•1 payment voucher exists
- **Fail Criteria**: No voucher
- **What to verify**: Voucher matches request amount, correct supplier

**Check 6: Within Budget**
- **Purpose**: Ensure no budget overrun
- **Pass Criteria**: Expenditure within allocated budget
- **Fail Criteria**: Exceeds budget
- **What to verify**: Budget validation passed during approval

**Activities:**
- Review sample transaction together
- Go through each check
- Discuss edge cases

**Practice Exercise:**

```
Transaction: GE-2025-000234
Amount: K15,500
Requester: ICT Department

‚úì 3 Vendor Quotes: PASS (3 quotes uploaded)
‚úó Proper Authorization: FAIL (only 1 approval found)
‚úì Budget Line Valid: PASS
‚úì Justification Provided: PASS (125 characters)
‚úì Payment Voucher: PASS
‚úì Within Budget: PASS

Question: Should this be flagged? What severity?
Answer: YES - Flag as MEDIUM (authorization issue)
```

#### **4. Flagging Transactions (10 minutes)**

**Topics:**
- When to flag a transaction
- Severity levels: Low, Medium, High, Critical
- Writing effective audit notes
- Following up on flagged items

**Flagging Criteria:**

**LOW Severity:**
- Minor documentation issues
- Missing non-critical information
- Formatting errors

**MEDIUM Severity:**
- 1-2 compliance checks failed
- Missing vendor quote
- Weak justification

**HIGH Severity:**
- 3+ compliance checks failed
- Missing authorization
- Significant policy violation

**CRITICAL Severity:**
- Suspected fraud
- Major budget overrun
- Missing approvals on large amounts

**Writing Audit Notes - Best Practices:**

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Be specific and factual
- Reference policy/procedure violated
- Suggest corrective action
- Include evidence (document names, dates)

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Make assumptions without evidence
- Use accusatory language
- Include personal opinions
- Write vague notes

**Example Audit Notes:**

**Good:**
```
Compliance Check Failed: Proper Authorization
Only 1 approval recorded (Line Manager) for K15,500 request.
Per policy, amounts >K10,000 require Bursar + ProVC approval.
Missing: Bursar approval, ProVC approval.
Recommendation: Review authorization workflow, implement
automated routing validation.
```

**Bad:**
```
This request wasn't approved properly. Someone made a mistake.
```

**Activities:**
- Review good vs. bad audit notes
- Practice writing clear notes
- Discuss as group

#### **3. Understanding Status Colors (3 minutes)**

| Icon | Status | Color | Meaning | Your Action |
|------|--------|-------|---------|-------------|
| ‚úì | Completed | Green | Step finished | None - wait for next |
| ‚è≥ | In Progress | Blue | Currently here | Wait for approval |

| ‚úó | Rejected | Red | Denied here | Review denial reason |

**What to Look For:**

- Green checkmarks (‚úì) - already completed
- Gray circles (‚è∏) - waiting to start

**Example:**
```

‚è∏ Bursary Processing (Gray) - Not started
‚è∏ Payment Completed (Gray) - Not started
```

**Translation:** Your request is with the Bursar for approval right now.
